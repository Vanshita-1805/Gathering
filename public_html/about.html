About The Event

After the boring COVID-19 pandemic of almost 2 years, College of Engineering Pune is back with "Antarang", which is COEP's annual gathering event. A college is marked by its great events that includes its annual social gathering. Antarang is a 3-day festival of the college that includes various events wherein students are given chance to showcase their talent.

"Antarang" means the talent you have within yourself. COEP Gathering helps you to promote your skills. It gives you chance to think, step forward and showcase your talent.

All events have their own speciality and are very unique in their own way. These events include Magnificent Drama, Splendid Variety Performances, Beautifully crafted AnC Exhibition, Street Play, Brainstorming Quizzes to stimulate your brain, COEP's Got Talent to find Prodigy of COEP, DJ Nights to make you Groove, Traditional Day to conserve our Indian culture and a lot more.

To promote sportsmanship in students, Gathering Sports are also organized which include Kabaddi, Volleyball, Box Cricket, Tug of War, Badminton, Table-Tennis, Indoor-Rowing, Athletics, Carrom, Chess and several other Fun Events.
