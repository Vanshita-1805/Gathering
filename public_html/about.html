About The Event

Get ready for the most awaited event of the year as the College of Engineering Pune presents "Antarang", its annual extravaganza! An event that showcases the pinnacle of talent and creativity, Antarang is a three-day celebration of art, music, and culture that promises to be an unforgettable experience. This much-awaited festival provides a platform for students to showcase their skills and unleash the talent that lies within them. With a name that means "the talent within you", Antarang is the perfect opportunity for you to take that step forward and let your skills shine. So come, join the festivities and let the College of Engineering Pune Gathering be your gateway to success!

Get ready to be mesmerized by the plethora of events that the College of Engineering Pune Gathering has to offer! Each event is a masterpiece in its own right, showcasing a unique blend of creativity and talent. From the magnificent drama performances to the splendid variety shows, and from the beautifully crafted AnC Exhibition to the thought-provoking quizzes, there is something for everyone. COEP's Got Talent is the perfect platform for budding artists to showcase their talents, while the DJ Nights will make you groove to the beats. Traditional Day is a tribute to our rich Indian culture and is a sight to behold. Sportsmanship is also given due importance at the Gathering, with a range of sports events organized, including Kabaddi, Volleyball, Box Cricket, Tug of War, Badminton, Table-Tennis, Indoor-Rowing, Athletics, Carrom, Chess and many more fun events. Whether you're a sports enthusiast or a cultural connoisseur, the College of Engineering Pune Gathering has something that will keep you entertained throughout!
